About us

Vision

In a world where people are constantly choosing the new over the old, we believe that by implementing aspects from the past into our modern lives, we can combine the best of two worlds and create something unique.

Message

Groovy believes that we must learn from the past in order to be successful and and be our true selves in the futre. That is why our messeage is: “Look back to step forward”.

Values

Authenticity. Creativity. Growth. Fun. These are the values that shape Groovy. We strive to help you discover your most authentic self in a community that values positivity and acceptance.

Unique Value Proposition

Groovy offers a concept that is very rare in todays market. We do not offer only the product and it’s packaging but also a piece of history with every piece of make-up. With Groovy you finally be your true self and express who you are through our creative products. We offer you encouraging and positive messages with every product to help you feel at your best!
